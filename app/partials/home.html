Who am I?

I was born in Budapest, Hungary. I started to learn software engineering in Secondary School, we started with Pascal and Delphi. I enjoyed it a lot, opened up the world from a whole different perspective. Started to see things from a different angle. Then while exploring this way of life I always had a softer side of me, a side that was way closer to humanity. I love interacting with people, teaching each other, learning from each other, understaing where ones come from and building something new and fascinating. I found design. All my life I have been looking for ways to create something that no one created before or not that way, to help people, to open up new doors. I failed a lot and to this day I still keep failing sometimes. But I achieved a lot. I still have sleepless nights where I try to figure out how to solve a problem, how to make visuals that speaks to everyone yet no one did before, how to make things work in ways that I imagined. How to tell a story, make people believe, cry, laugh, fear, depressed, obsessed, think... How to make my teammates life more easy, how to work better together. Trying out new tools, workflows, learn. Love creating and learning. That's me.
